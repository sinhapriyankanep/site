About Me

Welcome to my page! I am Priyanka, a final semester graduate aerospace student at the Israel Institute of Technology (Technion). My research focuses on advanced measurement techniques in thermo-fluids dynamics, multi-phase flows, and fundamental fluid mechanics of liquid sheet jets. Explore my site to learn more about my work and interests.

Research

Hypersonic Leading Edges

My MSc project involves developing cooling techniques for hypersonic vehicle leading edges. I focus on creating liquid sheet jets to achieve efficient cooling, utilizing advanced experimental facilities and shadowgraph imaging for precise characterization.

Liquid Sheet Jet Dynamics

I investigate the dynamics of liquid sheets generated by polyimide sheet nozzles, analyzing the impact of nozzle irregularities on sheet morphology. This research has significant implications for cooling systems in extreme environments.
